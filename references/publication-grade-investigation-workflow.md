# Publication-Grade Investigation Workflow

1. Mission brief.
2. Editorial line charter.
3. Source map.
4. Hypothesis and subclaims.
5. Evidence ledger.
6. Claim ledger.
7. Entity graph.
8. Timeline.
9. Countercase.
10. Legal risk.
11. Ethics check.
12. Draft.
13. Copy desk.
14. Red-team.
15. Ship gate.
16. Social/distribution package.
17. Post-publication log.

Every stage produces an artifact that can be linked in GBrain.
