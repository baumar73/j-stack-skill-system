# Source Hierarchy

1. Direct primary record: court judgment, statute, official register, original document, dataset with provenance.
2. Official assertion: agency statement, sanctions listing, press release. Strong for proving the agency position; not always proof of underlying facts.
3. Direct participant statement: interview, email, public statement. Strong for the speaker's position; not necessarily truth of underlying claim.
4. Independent expert analysis.
5. Reputable journalism with named sources/documents.
6. OSINT artifact observed directly.
7. Commentary/opinion.
8. Anonymous or recycled claims.

Always state what a source proves and what it cannot prove.
